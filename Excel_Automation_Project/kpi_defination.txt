KPI Name: Total Sales Revenue
Formula: Sum of Sales
Business Meaning:
Represents the overall revenue generated during the selected time period.
Used by management to evaluate overall business performance.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Total Orders
Formula: Count of unique Order Numbers
Business Meaning:
Shows the total number of customer orders placed.
Helps assess demand volume and customer activity.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Total Quantity Sold
Formula: Sum of Quantity Ordered
Business Meaning:
Indicates the total number of units sold.
Useful for inventory planning and supply chain decisions.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Average Order Value (AOV)
Formula: Total Sales / Total Orders
Business Meaning:
Measures the average revenue generated per order.
Helps evaluate pricing strategy and customer purchasing behavior.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Top Products by Sales
Formula: Group by Product Code and sum Sales
Business Meaning:
Identifies high-performing products contributing the most revenue.
Helps management focus on profitable products.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Top Countries by Revenue
Formula: Group by Country and sum Sales
Business Meaning:
Shows which geographical regions generate the highest revenue.
Supports regional sales strategy and market expansion planning.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Sales Trend (Yearly / Monthly)
Formula: Aggregate Sales by Year and Month
Business Meaning:
Tracks sales growth or decline over time.
Helps in forecasting and seasonal demand analysis.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Deal Size Contribution
Formula: Sales by Deal Size category (Small, Medium, Large)
Business Meaning:
Analyzes revenue contribution by deal size.
Supports decision-making for pricing and sales targeting strategies.

--------------------------------------------------

KPI Name: Best Performing Year
Formula: Year with highest total Sales
Business Meaning:
Identifies the most successful business year.
Used for performance comparison and future planning.
